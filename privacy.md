# MeowTiers Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information MeowTiers may process while operating as a Discord bot.

## 2. Data Collected

MeowTiers may temporarily process the following information in order to respond to commands:

- Discord usernames
- Discord user IDs
- Message command inputs
- Server IDs where the bot is used

This information is only used to provide the bot's functionality.

## 3. Data Storage

MeowTiers may temporarily cache certain data such as OCETiers player profiles to improve performance and reduce API requests.

This data is automatically removed after a short period of time.

The bot does not maintain permanent databases of personal user information.

## 4. Data Sharing

MeowTiers does not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.

The bot does rely on third party services including the OCETiers API.

## 5. Security

Reasonable efforts are made to ensure the bot operates securely. However, no service can guarantee complete security.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the bot indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
